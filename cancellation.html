Cancellation Policy

Appointment Cancellation

We value your time and ask that you value ours. To maintain a fair booking system and respect all clients, please follow these guidelines:

Cancellation Notice

  • Please provide at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel your appointment
  • This allows us to offer the time slot to other clients
  • Repeated last-minute cancellations may affect future booking availability

Rescheduling

  • Need to reschedule? No problem! Just let us know in advance
  • We'll work with you to find a new time that works for both of us
  • Please try to give at least 24 hours notice for rescheduling

No-Shows

If you miss your appointment without notice:

  • You may be required to schedule a consultation before booking future appointments
  • Future appointments may require additional scheduling verification
  • Please be courteous and let us know if you can't make it

Late Arrivals

  • 15+ minutes late: Your appointment may need to be rescheduled
  • 30+ minutes late: The appointment will need to be rescheduled
  • Please call if you're running late: (651) 592-5122

Exceptions

We understand that life happens. In cases of:

  • Medical emergencies
  • Severe weather conditions
  • Family emergencies

Please contact us as soon as possible, and we'll work with you to reschedule your appointment.

Payment Policy

We operate on an in-person payment basis:

  • All payments are made in person at the time of your appointment
  • We accept cash and major credit cards
  • Price quotes are provided during consultation
  • Final pricing may vary based on size, complexity, and time required
