Cancellation Policy

Appointment Cancellation

We understand that circumstances may arise requiring you to cancel or reschedule your appointment. To maintain a fair and efficient booking system, please adhere to the following policies:

Cancellation Notice

  • 48+ hours notice: Full refund of deposit
  • 24-48 hours notice: 50% of deposit refunded
  • Less than 24 hours notice: No refund of deposit

Rescheduling

  • First reschedule (with 48+ hours notice): No additional fee
  • Subsequent reschedules: May require a new deposit
  • Last-minute reschedules (less than 24 hours): Treated as cancellations

No-Shows

If you fail to show up for your appointment without any notice:

  • You will forfeit your deposit
  • Future bookings may require a larger deposit
  • You may be required to pay in full before booking future appointments

Late Arrivals

  • 15+ minutes late: May need to reschedule
  • 30+ minutes late: Considered a no-show
  • Please call if you're running late

Exceptions

We understand that emergencies happen. In cases of:

  • Medical emergencies (with documentation)
  • Severe weather conditions
  • Family emergencies

Please contact us as soon as possible to discuss your situation.

All appointments require a deposit to secure your booking. The deposit amount varies based on the size and complexity of the tattoo. This deposit:

  • Ensures your commitment to the appointment
  • Is subject to the refund policy outlined above
